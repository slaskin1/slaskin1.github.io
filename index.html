My name is Soren Laskin and I'm a recent graduate of Pitzer College in Claremont, CA. I'm currently pursuing my Masters in Management Information Systems and am scheduled to complete my degree in December.

I bring a broad set of both technical and interpersonal skills, and am looking to utilize these skills to contribute to more good in the tech industry. I believe nothing is siloed, and interdisciplinary learning is increasingly important in the age of AI and automation.

In our competition-driven society, we often prioritize innovation without fully considering long-term impacts. The industrial revolution transformed transportation and supply chains, but only later did we realize it had environmental costs, like damage to the ozone layer. Today, AI is evolving at a similar pace. Its prescence is already widespread, so now we must ask: where do we go from here?

With a background in sociology and organizational studies, alongside my technical graduate training, I'm interested in working with companies to explore how to harness AI responsibly and effectively. I'm passionate about making things more efficent and finding ways to guide innovation thoughtfully with a long-term global perspective.
