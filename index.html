My name is Soren Laskin and I'm a recent graduate of Pitzer College in Claremont, CA. I'm currently pursuing my Masters in Management Information Systems and am scheduled to complete my degree in December.

I have a wide variety of skills, both technical and soft, and am looking to utilize these skills to contribute to more good in the tech workforce. I believe nothing is siloed, and interdisciplinary learning is increasingly important in the age of AI and automation.

Under our competition-driven society, we have a tendency to innovate first and ask questions about long-term impacts after. The industrial revolution was huge for supply chain workflows and transportation, but it wasn't until years later it was discovered we tore a hole in our ozone layer. Similarly, AI is the next emerging technology, and the toothpaste is already out of the tube, so where do we go from here?

With my background in sociology and organizational studies, coupled with my more technical Masters, I want to explore options with companies about how to utilize AI in the best ways possible, while figuring out what next steps will be in AI innovation globally.
